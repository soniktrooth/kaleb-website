Kaleb's Comic Co.

Welcome to my comic company

My company started when I was in kindegarten I didnt know it but now I do.

When I was in kindy I only made books with pictures but no words.

My teacher (Ms. watt) would let me stay in at certain times to finish my previous books I was working on.

When I was in grade 1 I started writing words in my story to show what the character was doing.

In grade 2 I wasn't really much of a book maker but I practiced drawing that whole year (I mean I did do school work though the year as well but I did mostly just practice drawing.)

In grade 3 I started making prototype books with only like 3 to 6 pages and a lot of spelling mistakes.

In grade 4 I started making and even selling my own comic books.
